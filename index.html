DATASET
This Data Device uses observations and Q&A obtained through various staff memebers at our three food locations at UCL East to get data on food/menu items and queue wait times

DATA DEVICE (Physical)
 The data is visually represented using a color-coding system and a plain language description of various noise threshold. This approach aids users in understanding the impact of noise on their health.

DATA DEVICE (Digital)
 The Digital Twin of the data device provides additional information. It includes the precise noise level and an interactive chart describing the distribution of noise throughout the day, week, and month for different locations. The displayed information changes based on the userâ€™s selection.

HOW TO
 Open the App and point it at the screen of the Data Device. The Augmetned information will overlay with the Data Device, tap on the charts to change the visualisation from day to month. Use the dropdown menu to change space.
