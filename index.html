Gauge and Go

DATASET
This Data Device uses observations and Q&A obtained through various staff memebers at our three chosen food locations at UCL East to get data on menu items and queue wait times

DATA DEVICE (Physical)
 The data is visually represented usiing three buttons that allow the user to pick thier desired loction the wait time is then represented in a gauge in increaments of 0-5mins , 5-10 mins and 10+ mins and the spcial of the day and its price is represented in a LCD screen

DATA DEVICE (Digital)
 The Digital Twin of the data device provides additional information in three dashboards . Each dashboard includes a line graph that displays the peak wait times for each location and a table that displays daily specials for the week , a discreption of the food , calories , allergins , and an estimated serving time . the digital twin also includes a digital version of the gauge The dashboards changes based on the userâ€™s selection via a button .
